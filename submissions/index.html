Process

How to Submit in 5 Steps

  • Download the template. Get the official Manuscript Template above.
  • Prepare your manuscript. Write your paper following the formatting and structure in the Guidelines. Prepare an anonymized version for review, and fill out the Title Page Template separately.
  • Submit. Send your manuscript, title page, and cover letter to the editorial office.
  • Peer review. Your submission undergoes double-blind peer review. The editorial office will contact you with reviewer comments and any required revisions.
  • Publication. Once accepted, your paper is copyedited and published — freely available worldwide under our Open Access commitment.
Questions about formatting, eligibility, or the review process? Read the full Author Submission Guidelines above, or contact the editorial office — we're glad to help.
