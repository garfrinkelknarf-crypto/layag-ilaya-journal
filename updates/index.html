Announcement Now Open

Call for Submissions — Volume 2, 2027

Layag Ilaya Research Journal invites students, teachers, researchers, and community partners — inside and outside Ilaya Barangka Integrated School — to submit original research, action research, innovations, and scholarly work on intangible cultural heritage, Filipino local spirits marketing & innovation, AI & technology, and sustainability & community development.

  • Abstract submission deadline: August 31, 2026
  • Full paper submission deadline: November 30, 2026
  • Publication: March 2027

Submit via email at layagilaya@gmail.com or through our Facebook page.

Ongoing July 2026

Volume 2 Proposal Stage Underway

A new batch of Grade 12 researchers has begun drafting study proposals for Volume 2, working through statements of the problem, research design, and ethical review under faculty guidance before fieldwork begins.

Study Proposal Promo Videos
